Privacy Policy

Your privacy is my priority

I want you to feel safe using JoliTree. This policy explains what data the app uses and, more importantly, what it doesn't do.

Who I am

App: JoliTree
Creator: Eugene Evgen
Contact: eugene.evgen.contact@gmail.com

Your Data Stays On Your Device

I designed JoliTree to be a private, offline-first app. All the information regarding your tree customizations and ornament placements is stored locally on your device.

  • I do not have servers that collect your usage data.
  • I cannot see how you decorated your tree.
  • If you delete the app, your local settings are deleted from your device.

Third-Party Services

To provide certain features, I use a few trusted third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies and only have access to the specific data they need to function.

  • Apple: Handles all payment processing securely. I never see your payment information.
  • RevenueCat: Helps me verify subscription status (if applicable) and restore purchases. To do this, RevenueCat uses an anonymous User ID.

Anonymous Diagnostics

To find and fix bugs, the app may collect anonymous Crash Data and Performance Data. This information is not linked to your identity and helps me improve the app's stability.

Children's Privacy

JoliTree is not directed to children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If I make changes to this policy, I'll update this page and change the date below.

If you have any questions about your privacy, please don't hesitate to email me.
