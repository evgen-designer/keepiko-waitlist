Privacy Policy

Your privacy is my priority

I want you to feel safe using Keepiko. This policy explains what data the app uses and, more importantly, what it doesn't do.

Who I am

App: Keepiko
Creator: IEVGEN KUZNIETSOV
Contact: keepiko.info@gmail.com

Your data stays on your device

I designed Keepiko to be a private, offline-first app. All the information you enter - your rooms, spaces, boxes, items, and photos - is stored only on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework.

  • I do not have servers that collect your inventory data.
  • I cannot see what you've stored or look at your photos.
  • If you delete the app, all of its data is deleted from your device.

Backups

You have the option to create a backup of your data. This backup file is saved to a location you choose (like iCloud Drive or your computer) and remains completely under your control. I never have access to it.

In-app purchases

To manage Keepiko Plus subscriptions and purchases, I use two trusted services:

  • Apple: Handles all payment processing securely. I never see your payment information.
  • RevenueCat: Helps me manage your subscription status (like knowing if you're a Plus user) and restore your purchases on a new device. RevenueCat uses an anonymous ID and never has access to your personal information or your inventory data.

Children's privacy

Keepiko is intended for users 13 years of age and older. I do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If I make changes to this policy, I'll update this page and change the date below.

If you have any questions about your privacy, please don't hesitate to email me.
